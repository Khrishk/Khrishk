**Opinio** is an **AI & ML-powered analytics platform** designed to help **restaurants** gain deeper insights into their customer feedback.

🔹 **What Opinio Does**  
✅ Aggregates customer reviews from **Yelp, Google, TripAdvisor, Zomato**, and more  
✅ Uses **Natural Language Processing (NLP)** (NLTK & SpaCy) for **sentiment analysis**  
✅ Provides **actionable insights** to improve customer experience  
✅ Automatically **replies to customer reviews** with AI-generated responses that match the **restaurant’s unique tone & voice**
